Incendium Academy

We are a non-profit education platform that aims to level the playing field for high school students who cannot afford STEM contest preparation.

Why we started this

5/6 of Canada’s 2019 International Math Olympiad team had out of school training. This isn’t an exception, the other STEM contests look like this too.

We believe that one’s ability to pay should not determine success. Everyone should have a fair chance at doing well.

How it works

Flexible learning, so that anyone seeking to learn can follow the material A comprehensive curriculum made in collaboration with teachers and contest winners.

Who we are

Our content writing team is highly qualified in these contests. From a two time IOAA qualifier to an IOI silver medalist, our content is made by people who’ve found success and can help you find it too.
