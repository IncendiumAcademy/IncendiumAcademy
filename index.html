Incendium Academy

An education platform that aims to level the playing field for high school students who cannot afford STEM contest preparation.

A Message from the Founders

For the past few months, we formed a team of like-minded peers and have been developing an education platform to provide free resources for those competing in high school contests. We were inspired to take action in our community when we noticed the large disparities between the performances of those who were able to afford preparation classes and those who could not.

The purpose of our platform is to bridge this gap. Our website is open to anyone seeking to learn, and anyone seeking to teach can contribute. It specifically focuses on problem solving techniques used in advanced math, physics, and chemistry contests, making it unique from other similar platforms. This project is funded by the #RisingYouth grant, a program led by TakingITGlobal, which helps youth in Canada give back to their community.

Stay tuned for development updates, and more by following our socials.

Jae Park, Om Patel, Abhi Nair.
